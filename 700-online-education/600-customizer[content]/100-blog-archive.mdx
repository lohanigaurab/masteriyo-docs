# How to manage the Blog/Archive page?

Online Education theme includes the following options to customize your blog and archive pages.

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive

## Available Options 

## Layout

### How to manage the layout of the Blog/Archive page?

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive** section.

- Choose the number of Column as per your requirment for Blog/Archive page.

- Click on **Publish**.

![Layout Columns](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184545488-0c309ffc-3506-4c0e-ac39-9f9d423bf966.gif)

## Post 

### Available Options

- **Featured Image Size**: It allows you to choose options to manage featured image size.
    - Thumbnail (150*150)
    - Medium Large (768*0)
    - large (1024*1024)
    - Full

    ![Featured Image Size](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184545579-58d3529b-b1d6-4790-872e-56d5852c3f40.png)

- **Featured Image Ratio**: It allows you to choose options to manage featured image ratio.
    - Original
    - 4:3

    ![Featured Image Ratio](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184545627-bc76cf4e-882c-4542-938e-eb9866ebc4ed.png)

- **Excerpt Length**: It allows you to add a number for excerpt to display a contents as per your requirement.
    
    ![Excerpt Length](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184545668-8e824df1-6764-440b-a5d3-b1699baa5ca5.png)

- **CTA Text**: It allows you to add a text for Call to Action.

    ![CTA Text](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184545715-c4af8d40-d9ef-44d3-a20e-826e87ae3ca3.png)

- **Post Elements**

### To change the order of Post Elements on the Blog/Archive page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive**.

- Click on the Eye Icon will hide the content in the display window.

- Click on **Publish**.

![Blog_Archive-Post Content](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184546154-4491b224-7ab3-47c9-8665-352826466a5e.png)

- **Meta**

### To change the order of meta tags of Blog/Archive page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive** section.

- Click on the Eye Icon will hide the content in the display window.

- Click on Publish.

![Blog_Archive-Meta Tag](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184546258-9aaf905e-0b53-477b-a035-84d7c881083f.png)


- **Pagination**

### How to enable Pagination in the Blog/Archive page?

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive** section.

- Toggle **ON/OFF** the Enable Read Pagination option.

- Click on **Publish**.
