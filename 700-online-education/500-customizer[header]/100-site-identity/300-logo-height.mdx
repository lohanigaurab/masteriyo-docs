# How to change the Logo Height?

To change the logo height, follow the steps as mentioned below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Header > Site Identity** section.

- Find the **Logo Height** option and increase or decrease the height of logo as per your requirement.

- Click on **Publish**.
