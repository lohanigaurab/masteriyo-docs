# How to manage the Single Post content?

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Content > Single Post

This theme provides you the following options under the Single Post section:

## Title Postion: This option allows to choose an given option to manage the title position of the single post content.

### Available Options:

- Contents

- Page Header

## Posts

### Available Options

- **Featured Image Ratio**: It allows you to choose options to manage featured image ratio.
    - Original
    - 4:3

- **Post Elements**

#### To change the order of Post Elements on the Blog/Archive page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive**.

- Use drag-and-drop under Post Elements to give higher or lower priority to the items.

- Click on **Publish**.

- **Meta**

#### To change the order of meta tags of Blog/Archive page:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Content > Blog/Archive** section.

- Use drag-and-drop in the Meta Tags Order option to give higher or lower priority to the items.

- Click on Publish.

## Related Posts: It manages a related posts by given below options

- **Enable**: It allows you to enable the related posts in the single posts.

- **Columns**: Choose the number of Column as per your requirment to display a Related Posts in the single posts.
