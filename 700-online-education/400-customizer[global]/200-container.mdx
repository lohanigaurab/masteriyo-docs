## How to manage the Container of the site? 

Online Education theme allows you to manage the Container Width and Sidebar Width in the site.  

### Available Options

- **Container Width**: It allows to manage the Container width as per your requirment in the site.

- **Sidebar Width**:: It allows to manage the Sidebar width as per your requirment in the site.
