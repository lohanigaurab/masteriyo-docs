# How to manage Scroll to Top?

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Footer > Scroll to Top

You can customize Scroll to Top icon in the footer using the following options provided by this theme.

## Enable Scroll to Top

You can choose to enable or disable the Scroll to Top button to display in the Footer.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Scroll to Top** section.

- Toggle **ON/OFF** the Enable Scroll to Top option.

- Click on **Publish**.

## Colors: It allows to choose the Background and Icon color of the Scoll To Top button

- **Background**: Select the color of the background around the Arrow Icon in the Scroll to Top.

- **Icon**: Select the color of the icon around the Arrow Icon in the Scroll to Top.
