### How to Install Online Education Theme? - Free version: 

Online Education theme is really simple to install. You can instal the theme on your WordPress site basically by using two methods i.e., WordPress Dashboard and Upload Method. 

Let's go into detail about how to install Online Education theme. 

### Installing Online Education theme via WordPress Dashboard 

It is one of the simplest and fastest ways to install the Online Education theme on your WordPress Dashboard. Follow the instruction given below to install the Online Education theme. 

- Navigate to your **WordPress Dashboard > Appearance > Themes** and click on Add New. 

- From the search functionality on the WordPress dashboard, search for the Online Education theme. 

- Click on **Install** and **Activate** it. 

It is shown in the image below: 

![Online Education Theme via WordPress Dashboard](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/183817284-060d7bcd-cc1c-4100-b15c-0f5e1bc07b0c.png)

### Installing Online Education theme via Upload Method (Alternative Method) 

The upload method is suitable if you want to install the already downloaded Online Education them. In this method, you will need to upload the Online Education theme from your local computer to the WordPress dashboard. Follow the steps below to install the Online Education theme 

- Click on this link and a file named online-education.zip will automatically be downloaded to your computer. 

- After the download is completed, look for the online-education.zip file on your computer. It will likely be in your Downloads folder. Once you find it, remember the location and continue to the next step. 

- Go to **Appearance > Themes** and go to **Add New**. Now click on **Upload Theme**. 

- Click on browse and navigate to the downloaded online-education.zip file and click on Install Now. 

- Activate it.

![Install Online Education](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184284816-59904a22-5f83-4ed8-b7fd-5964e93e37d3.gif)


### Activate Online Education Theme 

In case if you missed activating the theme deactivate the Online theme, you can activate it manually with the following steps: 

- Go to **Appearance > Themes** in the WordPress Dashboard. 

- Hover over the Online Education theme thumbnail and click on the Activate button. 

- The theme is activated successfully
