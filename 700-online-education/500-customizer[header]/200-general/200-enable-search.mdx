#   How to enable search icon in the header?

In order to enable the Search Icon on your Header, follow these steps:

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > General**.

- Toggle **ON** the Enable option under Search section.

- Click on **Publish**.
