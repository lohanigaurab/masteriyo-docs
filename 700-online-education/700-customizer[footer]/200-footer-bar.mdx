# How to manage the Footer Bar?

    Location: Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Bar

Online Education theme allows you to customize the Footer Bar area using the options explained below:

## Copyright: 

It allows to add text under Copyright Section as per your requirment for the Footer Bar. Follow the steps as mentioned below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Footer > Footer Bar** section.

- Add text under Copyright Section as per your requirment for the Footer Bar

- Click on **Publish**.

![Copyright Text](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184547506-3ba108aa-8069-4dd0-bfbe-2c5661d984c6.png)


## Alignment: 

It allows to choose the aligment i.e Left, Centre and Right as per your requirment for the Footer Bar. 

![Copyright Alignmnet](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184547560-83d639f2-1a51-4303-b488-d485499d4422.png)


## Colors: 

It allows to manage Colors of the text as per your requirment for the Footer Bar.

### Available Options

- **Background Color**: It allows to choose a background color for the footer bar.

    ![Copyright Background Color](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184547609-e919231c-6a5e-448e-a123-b618a1aa0ba8.png)

- **Text Color**: It allows to choose a text color for the footer bar.

    ![Copyright Text Color](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/109593521/184547741-b9e3e3ab-1ae7-4815-af2c-4dec3a062ef6.png) 

- **Link Color**: It allows to choose a link color for the footer bar.
