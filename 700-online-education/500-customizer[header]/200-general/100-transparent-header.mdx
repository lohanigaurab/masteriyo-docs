#  How to enable Transparent Header of the site?

You can easily make header of your site transparent if you have the Online Education theme.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > General** section.

- Toggle **ON** the Enable under Transparent Header option.
