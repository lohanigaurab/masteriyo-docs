#  How to change the color of Site Title?

The Site Title color can be added and changed to best suit your website.

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to the **Appearance > Customize > Header > Site Identity** section.

- Choose the color of the Site Title to best suit your website

- Click on **Publish**.
