#   How to enable search in the site?

You can easily make header of your site transparent if you have the eLearning theme.

Just follow the steps below:

- From your **WordPress Dashboard**, go to **Appearance > Customize > Header > Transparent Header**.

- Toggle **ON** the Enable Transparent Header option.
